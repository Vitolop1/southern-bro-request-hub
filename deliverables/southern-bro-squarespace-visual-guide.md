# Southern Bro Enterprises Squarespace Visual Guide

## Goal

This guide translates the supervisor reference images into a cleaner, more usable website direction for Squarespace.

The site should feel:

- Bold
- Premium
- High-energy
- Brand-consistent
- Easy to read on mobile and desktop

## Visual Style Summary

The supervisor reference points to a very specific look:

- Dark cosmic background
- Purple and magenta glow
- Neon-style highlights
- Strong logo presence
- Premium gold accents
- Multi-brand service blocks

The website should keep that same mood while improving readability, spacing, and navigation.

## Core Design Direction

### 1. Background Style

Use layered dark backgrounds instead of flat white sections.

Recommended approach in Squarespace:

- Main backgrounds: black to deep purple gradients
- Selected sections: subtle galaxy texture or dark image with overlay
- Content cards: dark translucent purple blocks

## Section-by-Section Design

### Homepage Hero

Include:

- Southern Bro Enterprises logo
- Large headline
- One-line service summary
- Two CTA buttons

Recommended style:

- Dark purple/black gradient background
- Soft pink-purple glow behind the logo
- White text
- Purple CTA button
- Optional gold divider below hero text

### Brand Overview Section

Use a grid of service cards or alternating content rows.

Each card should include:

- Brand logo
- Brand name
- Short summary
- 3 to 5 service bullets
- CTA button

Recommended style:

- Dark cards with subtle purple border glow
- White headings
- Light text for bullets
- Accent color tied to the logo if needed

### Community Programs Section

Use a more formal visual tone for VA Recovery Services.

Recommended style:

- Dark background with navy and gold accents
- Highlight trust, service, and recovery support
- Include one clear CTA such as `Request Help` or `Learn About Programs`

### Contact and Final CTA

Use a strong closing section with:

- Phone
- Website
- Service area
- Main contact button

Recommended style:

- Deep black-purple background
- White text
- Purple or gold border line

## Squarespace Styling Notes

### Buttons

- Primary button color: `#C129FF`
- Primary button text: white
- Hover effect: slightly brighter pink-purple
- Corner style: medium rounded
- Avoid too many different button colors across one page

### Text

- Headings should be bold and large
- Use short paragraphs
- Keep bullet points concise
- Avoid putting long text over bright or busy image backgrounds

### Spacing

- Use more empty space than the flyer examples
- Keep one main idea per section
- Reduce text crowding, especially on mobile

### Images and Logos

- Use high-quality transparent PNG logos where possible
- Do not stretch logos
- Give each logo breathing room
- Keep logo sizes consistent across service blocks

## Recommended Homepage Look

1. Hero with Southern Bro logo and CTA
2. Company overview
3. Brands and services grid
4. How the request process works
5. Community support highlight
6. Service area and contact
7. Final CTA

## Design Do / Don't

### Do

- Use dark purple gradients
- Use strong contrast
- Keep the Southern Bro logo prominent
- Make service categories easy to scan
- Let each brand feel distinct but connected

### Don't

- Use a plain white corporate layout
- Overload one section with too much text
- Use weak contrast on dark backgrounds
- Make every section glow equally
- Copy the flyer layout too literally for mobile

## Best Way To Explain This To Your Supervisor

You can say:

`I used the flyer style as the visual direction for the website, but I am translating it into a cleaner Squarespace layout that keeps the same purple neon branding, logo emphasis, and service categories while improving readability, navigation, and customer usability.`
