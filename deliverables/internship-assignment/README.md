## Planning Assumptions

- Southern Bro Enterprises is the parent company.
- Current higher-priority growth areas are business consulting, detailing, ticketing, and customer intake.
- Southern Bro Landscaping and Southern Bro Creations / Wax Melt Bros remain part of the business, but they are treated as lower-priority website emphasis for the current rebuild phase.
- HubSpot is already in use and should remain the main CRM / lead-management system of record.
- No paid subscriptions should be activated without management approval.

## Recommended Delivery Order

1. Finalize website structure and navigation.
2. Rebuild the contact and quote intake flow.
3. Connect lead capture into HubSpot.
4. Add basic automation and follow-up workflows.
5. Add SEO content and resource pages.
6. Strengthen security, backups, and outage protection.
