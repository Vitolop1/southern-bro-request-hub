# Southern Bro Enterprises AI Tools Research

## 1. Objective

Identify practical ways AI can help Southern Bro Enterprises improve efficiency, customer support, content production, and internal operations without creating unnecessary cost.

## 2. Top 5 Recommended AI Use Cases

| # | AI Use Case | What It Could Do | Suggested Tools | Estimated Cost | Benefit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Customer intake assistant | Answer common questions, guide users to the right service, and collect initial inquiry details | ChatGPT, OpenAI API, HubSpot chat tools | Free research first; paid only with approval | Faster first response and cleaner lead intake |
| 2 | Proposal and email drafting | Draft follow-up emails, quote summaries, intake responses, and service explanations | ChatGPT, Claude | Free or low-cost business plan depending on use | Saves staff time and improves consistency |
| 3 | Marketing content generation | Draft blog posts, FAQs, social captions, and service page outlines | ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity for research support | Free to low-cost | Helps with SEO and regular content production |
| 4 | Research and competitor summaries | Summarize market trends, local competitors, and service ideas for management | ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity | Free to low-cost | Speeds up planning and reporting |
| 5 | CRM note summarization | Turn call notes or intake details into clean summaries and next steps | ChatGPT or API workflow | Depends on chosen workflow | Makes follow-up faster and clearer |

## 3. Tool Comparison

| Tool | Best Use | Free Option | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ChatGPT / OpenAI | Flexible drafting, customer support concepts, workflow building, API expansion | Limited free options may vary by product; business and API pricing are available | Best overall fit for future custom automations |
| Claude | Clean writing and long-form summarization | Yes | Good for internal writing and summaries |
| Perplexity | Fast research and source discovery | Yes | Useful for research support, but verify important details |

## 4. Recommended Starting Use

### Start Now

- use AI to draft emails, service descriptions, FAQs, and blog outlines
- use AI to summarize research and operations notes
- test AI-assisted intake scripts before building a chatbot

### Start Later

- connect AI to customer chat or intake workflows
- connect AI to CRM notes or quote preparation

## 5. Guardrails

- Do not enter sensitive financial data, banking details, or private customer information into public AI tools without approval.
- Review all AI-generated content before publishing or sending it to customers.
- Keep AI as an assistant, not as a replacement for business judgment.
- Any paid plan or API account must follow the company's free-trial approval policy.

## 6. Recommendation

For Southern Bro Enterprises, the best immediate value from AI is in content drafting, response drafting, research support, and internal organization. A customer-facing chatbot should be considered later, after the website and lead system are stable.
